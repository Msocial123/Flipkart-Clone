What is E-Commerce?

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, refers to the buying and selling of goods and services using the internet. It enables businesses and individuals to trade online without the need for physical interaction. Popular examples include Amazon, Flipkart, and eBay.

Advantages of E-Commerce:

  • 24/7 availability – shop anytime, anywhere
  • Wider reach – global customer base
  • Cost-effective – reduced operational costs
  • More product options and price comparisons
  • Personalized marketing and recommendations

Disadvantages of E-Commerce:

  • Lack of physical interaction with products
  • Security risks such as data breaches
  • Dependency on internet and tech infrastructure
  • Shipping delays or logistical issues
  • Limited customer service in some cases

Benefits for Customers:

  • Convenience and time-saving
  • Better deals, discounts, and cashback offers
  • Easy product comparison across vendors
  • Access to reviews and ratings
  • Flexible payment options including EMI, UPI, and wallets
